What is Urengi?

Urengi is a technology business that has made it own operating system for all of its devices. The name Urengi comes from Orange. If you take the vowels A, E, I, O, U, Y and go from left to right and take the vowels in 'orange', O to U, A to E and E to I. There are appliances, cars and devices such as desk tablets and phones.

What is a Desk tablet?

A Desk tablet is a tablet designed to be built into a desk. It comes with a stylus and the there is a notebook program is like a notebook so you can take notes on the tablet. You can buy a home package which is just buying one desk tablet for home use or a business package which then the tablets come with preinstalled program to let you view all the screen. You will need to specify how much proctor tablets you want.

Urengi phone 4

Urengi phone version 4. It has no apps on the screen so you can see your background image. To access the apps you go to the top and press the floating circle button and you will see all the apps. Another feature is the taskbar which is a black circle in the bottom left corner which expands with every app you open to easily press the app to open.

Urengi phone 5

Urengi phone 5 the newest Urengi phone. it add a keyboard with a track pad that you can slide out, but you can only use the keyboard is landscape mode. The phone automatically goes into a desktop mode when the keyboard is being used. You can get a mouse for an extra $10.
